# Product Context

## Register

product

## Users
Students, hobbyists, makers, and young developers learning robotics, coding, IoT, and embedded systems. They access this app while studying or building hands-on electronics kits.

## Product Purpose
MakersFlow Mobile (Edodwaja) serves as an interactive learning platform and store where users can watch course lessons, complete quizzes, check leaderboard streaking progress, view news feeds, manage certificates, and purchase physical engineering kits.

## Brand Personality
Interactive, educational, and modern. Emphasizes clean UI layouts, snappy responsive interactions, and rewarding feedback loops (streaks, badges, certificates).

## Anti-references
- Cluttered, low-contrast, or hard-to-read text layouts in dark mode.
- Sluggish transition animations or non-interruptible loading cycles.
- Plain, default-styled alert boxes or unrounded boundaries.

## Design Principles
- **Responsive Feedback**: Every press action must feel snappy and acknowledged (e.g. haptic feedback, scale changes).
- **Legibility First**: Dynamic contrast mapping ensures body and secondary labels remain high-contrast on all background cards.
- **Cohesive Aesthetics**: Custom themed components replace default system overlays (such as custom alert dialog modals).

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Full dark/light mode theme support.
- Text contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 for standard labels.
- Reduced motion compatibility.
