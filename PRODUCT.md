# MakersFlow Product Overview

## Vision

MakersFlow is a mobile-first EdTech platform developed by **Edodwaja Pvt. Ltd.** to make engineering education more interactive, practical, and engaging.

The platform combines structured learning, hands-on engineering kits, assessments, progress tracking, and community engagement into a single learning experience.

---

# Mission

To help students build practical engineering skills through interactive learning, real-world projects, and hands-on experimentation.

---

# Target Audience

MakersFlow is designed for:

- School students
- College students
- Robotics enthusiasts
- Electronics hobbyists
- IoT learners
- STEM educators
- Makers and young developers

Whether learning independently or through an educational institution, users can access structured learning content and practical resources from one application.

---

# Core Product Pillars

- Browse courses

- Track learning progress

---

## Practice

---

## Build

- Robotics kits

---

## Grow

- Learning progress
- Achievements
- Certificates
- Leaderboards
- Notifications

This encourages long-term engagement.

# Product Principles

## Practical Learning

Learning should lead to real-world skills.

Students should be able to build projects rather than simply complete lessons.

---

## Mobile First

The primary experience is designed for mobile devices.

Navigation, interactions, and layouts are optimized for touch interfaces.

---

## Engagement

Learning should remain motivating through:

- Streaks
- Achievements
- Certificates
- Progress tracking

---

## Simplicity

Interfaces should remain clean, intuitive, and easy to navigate.

Complexity should never be exposed to the learner.

---

# Design Principles

The product follows several UI principles.

- Responsive interactions
- High readability
- Consistent visual language
- Dark and light mode support
- Accessible interfaces
- Smooth animations
- Immediate feedback

Every interaction should feel responsive and intentional.

---

# Accessibility

The platform aims to be accessible for all learners.

Goals include:

- Dark and light themes
- High contrast text
- Reduced motion support
- Consistent navigation
- Touch-friendly controls
